### Build a Telephone Number Validator
In the US, phone numbers can be formatted in many ways. Here are some examples of valid formats for US phone numbers:


	1 555-555-5555
    	1 (555) 555-5555
   	1(555)555-5555
   	1 555 555 5555
   	5555555555
    	555-555-5555
    	(555)555-5555
